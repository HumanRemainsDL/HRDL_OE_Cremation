Life of Christopher
The King [Dagnus] then ordered that an iron bench be brought that was twelve cubits long which was proportional to the stature of that man, and [Dagnus] ordered this to be set in the middle of the city and then he ordered that Saint Christopher to be bound there and he ordered that a most un-small fire be kindled beneath it and as soon as the flame of the fire was at its greatest heat, [Dagnus] ordered that ten containers full of oil be poured over it because he wished that the heat of the fire were crueller and fiercer for that holy man. The Saint Christopher then called to the Lord with a bright voice in the middle of that most cruel and that most un-small pyre of flame
